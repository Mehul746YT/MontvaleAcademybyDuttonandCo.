Governance & Policies

Montvale Academy is committed to maintaining the highest standards of integrity, quality, and respect in our learning environment.

Appeals Policy
Learners may appeal assessment results or certification decisions by submitting a written request. Appeals will be reviewed fairly and decisions will be communicated after review.
Complaints Policy
Montvale Academy welcomes learner feedback and complaints. All complaints will be handled fairly, respectfully, and used to improve services.
Course Review Policy
Course materials are reviewed regularly to ensure accuracy, relevance, and quality.
Data Protection Policy
Learner information is kept secure and used only for educational and administrative purposes.
Equal Opportunity Policy
Montvale Academy provides equal learning opportunities and does not discriminate against learners.
Malpractice Policy
Cheating, plagiarism, impersonation, or misuse of certificates may lead to investigation and action.
Reasonable Adjustments Policy
Reasonable support may be provided to learners where required while maintaining assessment standards.
Safeguarding Policy
Montvale Academy aims to maintain a safe and respectful learning environment for all learners.
Evaluation Policy
Learner feedback is collected and used to improve courses and learner experience.
Responsible Marketing Policy
Montvale Academy provides accurate information about its programs and avoids misleading claims.
