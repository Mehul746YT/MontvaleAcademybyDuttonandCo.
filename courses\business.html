Business Administration

Montvale Academy Assessment

📖 About This Assessment

This assessment evaluates your understanding of business administration concepts, including management, finance, marketing, operations, and strategic decision-making.

Candidates are expected to prepare independently using books, courses, and other educational resources before attempting the examination.

📚 Recommended Study Areas

  • Business fundamentals
  • Management principles
  • Accounting basics
  • Marketing strategy
  • Business ethics
  • Financial analysis
  • Organizational behavior

📝 Certification Exam

The exam contains application-based questions designed to test practical understanding.

  • 50 Questions
  • Passing Score: 85%
  • Certificate issued after passing
