🏆 CPD Accreditation & Quality Assurance

Montvale Academy is a registered CPD Provider with the CPD Group (Provider #790431). All courses and assessments delivered by Montvale Academy are developed in accordance with CPD standards to ensure they contribute meaningfully to the continuing professional development of learners.

  • All course content is reviewed annually by subject-matter experts to ensure relevance and accuracy.
  • Assessments are designed to test applied knowledge, not rote memorisation.
  • Certificates issued by Montvale Academy carry a unique verification ID that can be independently verified through our records system.
  • We maintain an internal quality assurance framework aligned with recognised CPD benchmarks.

📜 Academic Integrity Policy

Montvale Academy operates a strict zero-tolerance policy on academic dishonesty. All learners are expected to complete assessments independently and honestly.

  • No Collaboration During Exams: Assessments must be completed individually. Any evidence of collusion, copying, or sharing of answers will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Prohibition of AI-Generated Answers: The use of AI tools, chatbots, or automated answer generators during assessments is strictly forbidden and constitutes academic misconduct.
  • Identity Verification: Montvale Academy reserves the right to request identity verification before issuing a certificate. Certificates may be revoked if the identity of the learner cannot be confirmed.
  • Plagiarism: Any attempt to submit plagiarised content in any open-ended assessment component will be investigated and may result in permanent exclusion from the platform.
  • Sanctions: Violations of academic integrity may result in: (a) nullification of assessment results, (b) revocation of issued certificates, (c) permanent ban from all Montvale Academy programmes, and (d) notification to relevant professional bodies.

📄 Assessment & Certification Policy

Our assessment framework is designed to ensure that only learners who demonstrate genuine competence are awarded certification.

  • Pass Threshold: A minimum score of 85% is required to pass any certification exam. This high threshold ensures that certified individuals possess a robust understanding of the subject matter.
  • Question Bank: Assessment questions are drawn from a rotating question bank and are subject to regular updates. Questions are application-based and scenario-driven.
  • Unlimited Retakes: Learners who do not achieve the pass mark may retake the assessment. There is no penalty for retaking an exam; however, each attempt is logged for quality assurance purposes.
  • Certificate Validity: Certificates issued by Montvale Academy are valid indefinitely, unless revoked due to a breach of academic integrity.
  • Certificate Verification: Each certificate includes a unique Certificate ID (format: MV-XXXXXXXXXXXX). Employers and institutions may contact Montvale Academy to verify the authenticity of any certificate.

🔒 Data Protection & Privacy Policy

Montvale Academy is committed to protecting the privacy of its learners in accordance with applicable data protection regulations.

  • Data Collection: We collect only the minimum data necessary to deliver our services — specifically, learner names (for certificate generation), assessment scores, and certificate IDs.
  • Data Storage: All learner data is stored securely using industry-standard cloud infrastructure with encryption at rest and in transit.
  • Third-Party Sharing: Montvale Academy does not sell, rent, or share learner data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data may be shared with regulatory or accreditation bodies only when legally required or for verification purposes.
  • Right to Erasure: Learners may request the deletion of their personal data by contacting montvaleuniversity@gmail.com. Please note that certificate records may be retained for verification purposes.
  • Cookies & Analytics: This website uses Google Analytics to monitor traffic patterns and improve the user experience. No personally identifiable information is shared with Google.

⚖ Complaints & Appeals Procedure

Montvale Academy takes all complaints seriously and is committed to resolving disputes fairly and transparently.

  • Stage 1 — Informal Resolution: Learners are encouraged to first raise concerns via email to montvaleuniversity@gmail.com. We aim to acknowledge all complaints within 48 hours and resolve them within 14 working days.
  • Stage 2 — Formal Review: If a learner is not satisfied with the initial response, they may request a formal review. The matter will be escalated to the Academy's Quality Assurance Officer for independent investigation.
  • Stage 3 — External Referral: If the complaint remains unresolved, learners may refer the matter to the CPD Group or any relevant external body for mediation.
  • Assessment Appeals: Learners who believe their assessment was graded unfairly may submit an appeal within 30 days of receiving their result. Appeals must include a clear rationale for the challenge.

💰 Refund & Cancellation Policy

As all courses and assessments offered by Montvale Academy are currently provided free of charge, no refund policy applies at this time. Should paid programmes be introduced in the future, a comprehensive refund and cancellation policy will be published in advance.

🌐 Equal Opportunities & Accessibility

Montvale Academy is committed to providing equal access to education regardless of age, gender, ethnicity, religion, disability, or socio-economic background.

  • All course materials are designed to be accessible via standard web browsers on desktop and mobile devices.
  • We are continually working to improve accessibility in line with WCAG 2.1 guidelines.
  • Learners who require reasonable adjustments during assessments should contact us in advance at montvaleuniversity@gmail.com.
