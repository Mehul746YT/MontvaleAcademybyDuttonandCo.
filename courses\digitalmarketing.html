Digital Marketing & Analytics

Montvale Academy Assessment

📖 About This Assessment

This assessment evaluates your competence in modern digital marketing strategies and analytics techniques. It covers SEO, paid advertising, social media, conversion optimisation and data‑driven decision making.

Prepare using current industry resources before attempting the exam.

📚 Study Material & Recommended Reading

Thorough preparation is essential — the pass mark is 85%. Review the following topics and resources.

Search Engine Optimisation (SEO)

  • Keyword Research — tools (Google Keyword Planner, Ahrefs), intent mapping.
  • On‑Page Optimisation — meta tags, heading hierarchy, schema markup.
  • Technical SEO — site speed, mobile‑first indexing, crawl budget.
  • Link Building Strategies — outreach, content‑driven backlinks.

Paid Advertising (Google Ads & Social)

  • Campaign Structure — account hierarchy, ad groups, keyword match types.
  • Ad Copy & Extensions — ad copy best practices.
  • Bid Management & Smart Bidding — CPA, ROAS, automated bidding.
  • Social Media Ads — platforms and audience targeting strategies.

Conversion Optimisation

  • Conversion Funnels — user journeys and drop‑off analysis.
  • A/B Testing — hypothesis creation, statistical significance.
  • Landing Page Best Practices — copy, layout, and visual CTA design.

Analytics & Reporting

  • Google Analytics 4 — event‑based measurement, custom dimensions.
  • Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) — CAC, LTV, ROAS, and session metrics.
  • Dashboarding — compiling dynamic executive‑level reports.

Recommended Textbooks & Resources

  • SEO 2024 – Learn Search Engine Optimization by Adam Clarke
  • Google Ads (AdWords) Workbook by Jason McDonald
  • Digital Marketing For Dummies by Ryan Deiss & Russ Henneberry
  • Measure What Matters by Katie Paine (analytics & reporting)
  • Conversion Optimization by Khalid Saleh & Ayat Shukairy

The exam contains 20 application‑based questions covering the topics above.
